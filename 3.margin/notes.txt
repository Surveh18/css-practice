margin - imaginary space outside the container. not in the div or any element.
we can also give margin in negative direction but not in padding.
 
padding - imaginary space inside the container/box.

border-radius - use to make curve edger or to make circle.

border syntax:-
border - size(2px) | solid/dashed/dotted | color.

border - 2px solid blue  and it can also be none
